Kancheepuram, Tamil Nadu — to Dubai

Silk woven by hand, carried across the sea for you.

Pavnika by Saranya is a Dubai-based boutique bringing genuine Kanjivaram silk sarees to the UAE — each one handwoven in Kancheepuram, in real zari, across a vast and ever-growing collection spanning everyday weaves to bridal Kanjivaram.

See the Full Collection

We travel to Kancheepuram ourselves to sit with the weavers, and every saree in our collection is handpicked in person — nothing chosen from a catalogue we haven't seen. Beyond what's already woven, a number of our pieces are commissioned specially: a colour, a border or a motif we've requested, brought to life on the loom exclusively for Pavnika by Saranya.

“A Kanjivaram is not bought for one evening. It is chosen the way a family chooses an heirloom — to be worn, folded, and worn again for thirty years.”

— Pavnika by Saranya, on how we select each piece
