Who We Are

Pavnika Online Seller (Pavnika by Saranya), licensed by Dubai DET, license number 1563920, based in Al Barsha South 4, Dubai, UAE, is the data controller responsible for your personal data collected through this website.

What We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you use this website:

  • When you verify your email to enter the site: your email address and mobile number
  • When you submit an enquiry via our Contact page: your name, phone number, email address, the occasion you select, and any message you write

How We Use Your Data

We use this information to:

  • Verify visitors and prevent unauthorised or automated access to the site
  • Respond to your enquiries about our sarees
  • Contact you via WhatsApp, phone, or email regarding your enquiry

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for marketing without your separate consent.

Cookies

This website uses one functional cookie (pavnika_verified) to remember that you have completed email verification, so you are not asked again for 90 days. We do not currently use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Service Providers

To operate this website, we use the following service providers, who process data on our behalf:

  • Resend — for sending verification code emails
  • Supabase — for securely storing verification records
  • Netlify — for website hosting and for handling Contact form submissions

These providers may process data on servers located outside the UAE. We only work with providers that maintain industry-standard security practices.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain enquiry and verification data only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and maintain our business records, after which it may be deleted or anonymised.

Your Rights

Under UAE data protection law, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and to object to how it is processed. To exercise these rights, contact us at pavnikabysaranya@gmail.com or prichu.p@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as our practices or the law change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version.

Contact Us

For any privacy questions or requests, email pavnikabysaranya@gmail.com or prichu.p@gmail.com.
