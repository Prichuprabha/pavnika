Frequently Asked Questions

A genuine Kanchipuram (Kanjivaram) saree is handwoven in Kancheepuram, Tamil Nadu, using pure mulberry silk and real zari thread. Authentic pieces often carry a Silk Mark certification and show the slightly uneven, handloom texture that distinguishes them from machine-made or blended imitations.

Genuine silk has a soft texture, a natural (not artificial-looking) sheen, and warms slightly when rubbed between your fingers. We only source directly from weaving families in Kancheepuram, and are happy to share close-up photos or video of the weave and zari before you commit.
