Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: July 2026

Within the UAE

We offer free delivery anywhere in the UAE, with your saree arriving within 3 days of order confirmation.

Overseas Delivery

We also deliver internationally. Overseas orders are subject to additional shipping charges, calculated based on your location — we'll confirm the exact cost with you before your order is finalized.

Order Confirmation

Delivery timelines begin from the point your order is confirmed directly with us (via WhatsApp or in person), not from when an enquiry is first submitted.

Questions About Your Delivery
