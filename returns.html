Our Current Policy

At this time, Pavnika by Saranya does not offer returns or exchanges on purchased sarees. Because each piece is handled individually and often reserved specifically for a customer following direct discussion, all sales are considered final once confirmed.

Before You Confirm a Purchase

To help you feel confident before committing:

  • We're happy to share additional photos or a short video of the actual piece over WhatsApp
  • We encourage questions about weave, colour, and sizing before you confirm
  • Private viewings can be arranged where possible

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If an item arrives damaged in transit, or if you receive a different saree than the one confirmed, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at pavnikabysaranya@gmail.com or via WhatsApp, with photos of the item received. We will review each case individually and do our best to make it right.

Future Updates

As we introduce online payments and a more structured order process, this policy will be reviewed and updated accordingly.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to pavnikabysaranya@gmail.com.
